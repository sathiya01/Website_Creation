WHAT IS MEDITATION?

Meditation is not about becoming a different person, a new person, or even a better person.It is about training in awareness and getting a healthy sense of perspective. You are not trying to turn off your thoughts or feelings. You are learning to observe them without judgment.And eventually, you may start to better understand them as well.
